Comprehensive Health & Emergency Care Within Campus

Campus Care is your one-stop solution for all healthcare and emergency needs within the university. Whether you’re a student feeling under the weather, a staff member needing a quick check-up, or someone facing a minor emergency — we’re here for you. We provide on-campus doctor consultations, first-aid support, medicine stock tracking, and even allow borrowing essential medical or mobility equipment — all from the convenience of your university.
